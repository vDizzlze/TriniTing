About The Game

This game is played by selecting cards from the face down position until they have a matching set of cards (pair, triple or quadruple). The player starts by clicking a single card which will then turn face up. The player must then proceeds to click 1, 2, or 3 more cards based on the game setting of easy, medium or difficult respectively. If at any point in this sequence the player clicks on a non-matching card all cards currently face up will be returned to the face down position. Once a full matching set of cards have been selected they will remain face up.

Due to the game mechanic of getting points for the time remaining, the players with the highest APM's (Actions Per Minute) will score the highest.

Easy Difficulty: Pick 2 identical cards in a row, 3 minutes timer, time remaining is worth 1 point for each remaining second

Medium Difficulty: Pick 3 identical cards in a row, 2 minutes timer, time remaining is worth 5 points for each remaining second

Difficult Difficulty: Pick 4 identical cards in a row, 1 minute timer, time remaining is worth 10 points for each remaining second
